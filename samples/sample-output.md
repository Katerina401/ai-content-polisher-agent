# Sample Output

Target platform: WeChat public account article

## Diagnosis Result

1. Mechanical structure
   - The draft follows a predictable "background - importance - suggestion - conclusion" pattern.
   - Paragraph openings such as "在当下这个..." and "因此..." feel generic.

2. Vague expression
   - Phrases like "提升自己的能力" and "获得更好的发展" are broad and lack concrete context.

3. Over-explaining
   - The text repeatedly states that personal growth is important without adding new insight.

4. Preachy tone
   - "我们应该从现在开始行动起来" sounds like a slogan rather than an article voice.

5. Platform mismatch
   - For a WeChat article, the draft needs a clearer entry point, more specific scenes, and a warmer narrative rhythm.

## Optimized Version

```text
很多人说想成长，但真正卡住的地方，往往不是不知道要努力，而是不知道每天该把力气放在哪里。

比如下班后只有一小时，是继续刷信息流，还是把今天工作里没想明白的问题复盘一下？周末有半天时间，是临时焦虑地报一门课，还是先整理出自己最薄弱的一个能力点？这些选择看起来很小，却会慢慢拉开差距。

所谓个人成长，不一定要从宏大的计划开始。更实际的做法，是先找到一个具体问题：表达不清，就练一次复盘；效率低，就记录一周时间去向；心态容易被打乱，就给自己留出固定的整理时间。

每天只推进一点，短期看不明显，但它会让人逐渐摆脱空泛的焦虑。成长不是一句口号，而是把注意力一次次收回来，放到真正能改变现状的事情上。
```

## Self-Check

- Meaning preservation: The revised version keeps the original focus on personal growth and continuous improvement.
- AI-flavor reduction: Generic slogans and repetitive explanations are replaced with concrete daily scenes.
- Platform fit: The tone is more suitable for a WeChat public account article.
- Factual consistency: No unsupported data, personal identity, or external claims are added.
