# Project Overview

AI Content Polisher Agent is a documentation-first prototype for Chinese content quality optimization. The project is in the **prototype validation stage / 原型验证阶段** and is designed to explore how a multi-agent workflow can diagnose and reduce common "AI flavor" in Chinese drafts.

## Background

Chinese creators increasingly use AI tools to draft articles, posts, scripts, and prose. These drafts often contain useful structure but still feel generic, over-explained, or mismatched with the target platform. The project focuses on the editing layer after generation: identifying quality issues, preserving meaning, and improving naturalness.

## Target Users

- WeChat public account writers who need article-level polish and clearer paragraph rhythm
- Xiaohongshu creators who need concise, vivid, platform-native copy
- Short video teams who need more spoken, audience-facing scripts
- Fiction and prose writers who need natural narrative texture
- Marketing writers who need less template-like promotional language

## Core Pain Points

- Mechanical paragraph structure and predictable transitions
- Repetitive sentence rhythm across long-form drafts
- Vague claims without concrete scenes, examples, or wording
- Over-explaining that weakens reader engagement
- Preachy or summary-heavy tone
- Platform mismatch between article, social post, video script, and prose styles
- Risk of meaning drift when rewriting aggressively

## Application Scenarios

The prototype is intended to support AI-flavor diagnosis, naturalized rewriting, platform style adaptation, long-form consistency checks, and self-check review before final delivery.
